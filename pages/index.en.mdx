# Olynet

## General Information

Olynet is a student club that provides IT-Support regarding internet issues 
to students living in the dormitory "Olympisches Dorf" in Munich.
You can also purchase pre-configured routers during our opening hours.

### Opening Hours
* Every Monday 20-21 o'clock

Before visiting us on our opening hours during holidays, please check 
our social media presence. If we are closed, we will let you know there.

### Office 
You can find our office in the highrise (Helene-Mayer-Ring 7) in the former 
community room (GAP) in the 2nd story. If you live in a Bungalow, your key 
unfortunately only works for the staircase.
